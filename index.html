About Us

Welcome to Rising FC - Where Passion Meets Teamwork!

Established in 2022, Rising FC is a futsal team dedicated to promoting sportsmanship and excellence. Our team is built on the principles of hard work, unity, and an undying love for the game. We have competed in numerous local and regional tournaments, earning recognition for our talent and relentless spirit.

Our mission is to inspire the youth, build a community through futsal, and make our mark on the global stage. Together, we rise higher with every challenge, every match, and every goal!
